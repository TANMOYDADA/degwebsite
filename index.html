What it is

The grid moved electrons. DEG moves coordination.

Today, energy systems are electrically connected but digitally fragmented. DEG provides thin, open digital rails so actors can discover, verify, transact, settle and audit across networks.

A rooftop solar unit A battery A meter An EV charger A flexibility event A carbon attribute A tariff rule A settlement proof
